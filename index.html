Our Process

01

Initial Consultation

During our initial conversation, we'll focus on understanding your vision and needs to tailor a website solution that aligns perfectly with your business goals. We want to make sure that we are the best fit for each other.

02

Contract Agreement

Before we can proceed with your project, we'll provide a contract agreement that requires your signature. This step ensures both parties are aligned and ready to move forward.

03

Planning and Designing

This is the kickoff and analysis portion of our relationship. You will be presented with a Statement of Work that details all of the portions of content that both you and I are responsible for. As one of the best of Lubbock's web design agencies, we understand that content is the heart of your website.

04

Development and SEO

During the development phase, we transform your vision into a fully functional website, meticulously coding and integrating all necessary features. This technical foundation is then complemented by our SEO efforts to improve visibility on search engines.

05

Launch and Support

As we prepare to launch your website, our team meticulously reviews every detail to ensure perfection. Upon launch, your site will be live, ready to captivate and engage your audience. We offer free support hours to clients who opt to host their sites independently.

Featured Project: Fatherhood

A Full-Stack Community Platform for Fathers

Fatherhood is a comprehensive full-stack web application designed to support and connect fathers. Built with React and a Node.js/Express backend, it combines educational content, a dynamic quote system, and local community mapping to provide a vital resource for modern parenting.

The platform features secure user authentication, a curated library of articles, and an interactive Google Map displaying local men's groups and events in Lubbock, TX. It's a robust, scalable application that demonstrates advanced skills in both frontend and backend development.
