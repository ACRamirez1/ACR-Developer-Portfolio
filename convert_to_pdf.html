Method 3: Screenshot Method (Quick Alternative)

If you just need a quick PDF for your portfolio:

  1. Open your resume in browser
  2. Take a screenshot of the entire page (Ctrl+Shift+I to open dev tools, then Ctrl+Shift+P and type "screenshot")
  3. Or use Windows Snipping Tool / Mac Screenshot
  4. Save as image and convert to PDF using online tools

Method 4: Word/Google Docs

Copy and paste method:

  1. Open your resume in browser
  2. Select all content (Ctrl+A)
  3. Copy (Ctrl+C)
  4. Paste into Microsoft Word or Google Docs
  5. Format as needed
  6. Save/Export as PDF
